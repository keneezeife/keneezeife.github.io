INTRODUCTION

Born and raised in Nigeria, I am aware of my nation's pride in her many natural resources, from her rich oil wells to vibrant wildlife. I am also aware that Nigeria’s resources can be more efficiently harnessed if the nation were to invest in technology to assist the nation's already large work force.

Thus, I am passionate about technological growth. I believe that the field of engineering can help our globalized world to develop. Living towards this dream, I participated in Nigeria's Interswitch SPAK Innovation Challenge where my team and I suggested ways to improve Nigeria's public transportation system through an online application.

Specifically, I am interested in Mechanical Engineering, Economics and helping others in my community.

RIDDLES

Are you feeling lucky? Step right up and see if you can guess the answer to some of thee mind blowing riddles.

1) Q: What always ends everything???

A: The letter "G"

2) Q: A boy and a Doctor went fishing. The boy was the Doctor’s son but the Doctor wasn't the boy’s father. Who is the Doctor?

A: The boy’s mother

3) Q: I'm as light as a feather but the strongest of people cannot hold me for over a minute. What am I?

A: Breath

4) Q: What has one head, one foot and four legs?

A: A Bed

5) Q: Did you hear the joke about the roof?

A: Never mind, it's over your head!

6) Q: How many letters are in The Alphabet?

A: There are 11 letters in The Alphabet

7) Q: If you were in a race and passed the person in 2nd place, what place would you be in?

A: 2nd place!

8) Q: What is the center of gravity?

A: The letter V!

9) Q: What English word has three consecutive double letters?

A: Bookkeeper

10) Q: What has a head, a tail, is brown, and has no legs?

A: A penny!

11) Q: The turtle took two chocolates to Texas to teach Thomas to tie his boots. How many T's in that?

A: There are 2 T's in THAT!

12) Q: What gets bigger and bigger as you take more away from it?

A: A hole!

13) Q: How many months have 28 days?

A: All of them!

14) Q: How many books can you put into an empty backpack?

A: One! After that it's not empty.

15) Q: Does your shirt have holes in it?

A: No, then how did you put it on?

16) Q: What starts with a P and ends with an E and has a million letters in it?

A: Post Office

17) Q: What is full of holes but can still hold water?

A: A sponge!

18) Q: What tastes better than it smells?

A: A tongue

19) Q: Where does success come before work?

A: In the dictionary!

20) Q: What breaks when you say it?

A: Silence!
